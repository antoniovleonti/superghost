What is superghost?

Superghost (also known as Lexicant) is a word game where two or more players take turns adding letters to a word stem. The first player to spell a valid word loses the round. Players can also challenge if they believe a valid word cannot be spelled with the current word stem.

What's the point of this website?

One of the great things about superghost is that it can be played with no materials. Most people, even those who aren't especially wordy, can easily play without pencil and paper. Accessibility was not a motivation for creating the website. The most important one is just that I wanted to and knew I would learn a lot. Still, there are benefits to playing on the website, however. For example, you can enjoy a very intense, competitive experience when playing with a clock. Also, if you don't have any friends who enjoy playing superghost, you can meet people here to play and chat with in public games.
